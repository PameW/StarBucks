# ☕ Customer Review Analysis using NLP (Starbucks)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project applies Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to analyze customer reviews and extract meaningful insights about customer experience and sentiment.

The goal is to transform unstructured text data into actionable information that can support customer satisfaction analysis and business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Companies receive large volumes of customer feedback, but analyzing text data at scale is challenging.

This project helps answer:

- What are the main drivers of positive and negative customer experiences?
- How do customer sentiments relate to ratings?
- What patterns can be identified in customer feedback?

---

## 📊 Dataset

- Source: Kaggle
- Type: Customer reviews dataset
- Data includes:
    - Text reviews
    - Ratings
    - Customer opinions

---

## ⚙️ Methodology

The project follows a structured NLP workflow:

- Text cleaning and preprocessing
    - Tokenization
    - Lemmatization
    - Stopword removal
- Feature extraction
    - TF-IDF vectorization
- Sentiment analysis
- Exploratory text analysis
    - Word frequency analysis
    - Keyword extraction
- Relationship analysis between text and ratings

---

## 📈 Key Results

- Identified key terms associated with positive and negative reviews
- Analyzed sentiment distribution across customer feedback
- Found relationships between review content and rating scores
- Extracted insights into customer perception of products and services

---

## 💡 Business Impact

This type of analysis can help companies:

- Improve customer experience
- Identify common issues in products or services
- Enhance brand perception strategies
- Support decision-making in marketing and operations
